## 🤖 Problem 


Please create a dataset containing information about your friends of your chosen social media platform.
 
Instructions:
Collect 100 samples containing the information below. Save it as data.xlsx.
 
| Name   | Posts/Share Frequently? | Active? | Replies to comments/messages? | Number of Friends/Followers | Personality |
|--------|-------------------------|---------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|-------------|
| Elizer | 0                       | 1       | 1                             | 1000                        | 0           |
| Leon   | 1                       | 1       | 1                             | 2000                        | 1           |
	
 
 
After collecting the data, use the notebook below to train your first machine learning model to predict your friends personality.
